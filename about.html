What is fightpass.gg?

fightpass.gg is a quick reference point for tracking when each round of the Tekken 8 fight pass will begin and end. The site helps players keep track of the deadline for unlocking time-limited collectables.

Why was it created?

This site was created by Jared Rigby in 2025 to provide a simple, easy-to-access countdown timer for the Tekken 8 fight pass. Whether you're trying to complete your fight pass or just want to know how much time you have left, fightpass.gg gives you the information at a glance.

Features

  • Real-time countdown to fight pass end date
  • Progress bar showing how much of the fight pass period has elapsed
  • Start and end dates clearly displayed
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
