What is fightpass.gg?

fightpass.gg is a quick reference point for tracking when each round of the Tekken 8 fight pass will begin and end.

Why was it created?

This site was created by Jared Rigby in 2025 to provide a simple, easy-to-access countdown timer for the current Tekken 8 fight pass.
